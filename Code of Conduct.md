# Code of Conduct for Tech Growth Lab 🚀

We value an open and welcoming community for developers, SaaS founders, hardware enthusiasts, and tech innovators. All contributors and participants in **Tech Growth Lab** are expected to follow this Code of Conduct to ensure a safe and productive environment.

---

## 1. Be Respectful

- Treat everyone with respect and professionalism.  
- Respect differing opinions, experiences, and perspectives.  
- Avoid personal attacks, offensive comments, or harassment.

---

## 2. Be Inclusive

- Encourage participation from people of all backgrounds and experience levels.  
- Avoid discriminatory or exclusionary language.  
- Foster collaboration and supportive discussions.

---

## 3. No Spam or Self-Promotion

- Avoid unrelated promotion, ads, or excessive self-promotion.  
- Guest posts should align with **tech products, SaaS, software development, hardware, or growth strategy** topics.  

---

## 4. Maintain Professionalism

- Posts should be clear, concise, and constructive.  
- Use appropriate language — no offensive, inflammatory, or NSFW content.  
- Cite sources and provide credit where necessary.

---

## 5. Reporting Issues

If you encounter behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. Contact the repository maintainers via email or GitHub issues.  
2. Provide details about the incident, including screenshots if relevant.  
3. The maintainers will review and take appropriate action.

---

## 6. Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- Removal of posts or comments  
- Temporary or permanent banning from discussions  
- Reporting to GitHub in severe cases

Maintainers reserve the right to take action to ensure a safe and welcoming environment.

---

## 7. Acknowledgements

This Code of Conduct is based on the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), adapted for **Tech Growth Lab**.

---

> By participating in this repository, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct. Together, we can build a **friendly, professional, and thriving tech community**. 🚀
